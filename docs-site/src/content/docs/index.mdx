## What is it?

Northstar is a self-hosted iOS app that unifies three pillars of your life into one
Robinhood-grade dashboard, then lets you ask Claude questions like
*"Should I push hard today?"* or *"Am I on track for OSCP by April?"* against your own data.

<CardGrid stagger>
  <Card title="Finance" icon="approve-check">
    Mirrors your existing [Actual Budget](https://actualbudget.org/) instance.
    Per-category spend rings, threshold notifications (50/75/90/100%), purchase
    push, anomaly detection.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Goals" icon="rocket">
    Native milestone + daily-log + reminder system. Streak tracking. Daily brief
    notification. One-time CLI to seed from a Notion workspace.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Health" icon="heart">

    Today's verdict: push / maintain / recover.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Ask Claude" icon="sparkles">
    Chat tab with tool access to all three pillars. Prompt-cached system block.
    Mock mode default so you can run the full stack without an Anthropic key.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

## Why self-host?

- **Your data stays on your box.** Single SQLite file. Litestream replicates encrypted
  snapshots to your B2 bucket. Export to JSON anytime.
- **No telemetry.** No analytics SDKs. No third-party services in the iOS app.
- **Claude is opt-in.** Bring your own API key. Your data never leaves your server
  except for the specific prompt you sent.
- **MIT license.** Fork it, brand it, ship a version for your family. No legal review.

## Status
