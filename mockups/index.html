Notes on the design language

The aesthetic is anchored to three references: Robinhood for the pure-black, big-number, accent-color treatment of finance; WHOOP for the radial recovery dial and stop-light health palette; Credit Karma for the card-based modular layout that lets each pillar render on the home screen without crowding.

Color signals (load-bearing)
Green — under budget, recovery cleared, goal complete.
Amber — approaching threshold, recovery mid-band.
Red — over budget, low recovery, missed task.
Indigo — goals pillar accent.
Amber-orange — Claude / AI surfaces.
Typography
Inter (Apple SF Pro on device). Heavy weight contrast — Display 800 on the numbers, Regular 400 on the prose, Medium 500 on rows. Tabular figures so columns align.
What's intentionally absent
No tutorial overlays. No empty-state illustrations. No achievement badges. No social. No leaderboards. No "streak guilt" past a soft surface. The product trusts you to read your own numbers.
Iteration notes
v1 = these 5 tabs + lock-screen pushes. WidgetKit (net worth + recovery dial) and Live Activities (budget-threshold progress) land in Phase 2 / 3. Apple Watch app is Phase 7.

Open mockups/index.html in any browser. Full plan at docs/ULTRAPLAN.md.
